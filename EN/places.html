Mis Girasoles Beekeeping

A beautiful place to visit in Lunahuaná, where we can Learn more about how natural honey is produced from the hand of our friends of the Apicola Mis Girasoles, We can also buy natural honey or related products directly.

Santa Maria Winery

La Bodega Santa María is a family business that is operating since 1929, where we can enjoy the tasting of wines and piscos, In addition to being able to appreciate the views offered by the place and a tour for its wineries.

Reyna de Lunahuaná Winery

The Reyna de Lunahuaná Winery is another tourist place that we can visit, where we can also enjoy wine tasting, know how wines are produced and take a tour of its facilities.

Inca Huasi Complex

Incahuasi de Lunahuaná, known as the New Cusco, was built in the period of the Inca Pachacutec, on the left bank of the Cañete River, at an average height of 350 m.a.s.l. It could be used to receive and supply the Inca hosts, later becoming an important administrative center.

Church of Santiago Apostle

The Temple of Santiago Apóstol, is located in the Plaza de Armas of this district. The church, presents a building built following the Gothic style patterns; and inside that stands out the Main altarpiece of three bodies, in the center of which you can see the Image of the Apostle Santiago.

Main Square of Lunahuaná

The Main Square of Lunahuaná is a place to hang out and visit the attractions that are around since we can find restaurants, gift shops, monuments, among others.

Hanging Bridge

Built in the sixties with noble material and steel cables, This bridge had a previous model made with handcrafted materials which was easily washed away in one of the floods of the Cañete River. It is located in the annex of Catapalla, 15 minutes from Lunahuaná road to Yauyos.
